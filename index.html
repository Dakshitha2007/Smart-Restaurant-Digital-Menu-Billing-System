Our Story

Born Under Golden Skies,
Made for the Soul

Sunset Serenade is more than a restaurant — it's a feeling. The warmth of a setting sun, the aroma of spices, and the music of clinking glasses.

Who We Are

Ahmedabad's Golden Hour Restaurant

Founded in 2015, Sunset Serenade was born from a simple dream: create a space where vegetarian food is celebrated, not compromised. Perched on a rooftop in Bodakdev, we serve Ahmedabad's skyline alongside our dishes — both equally breathtaking.

Our kitchen draws inspiration from across India and the world — the smoky tandoors of Punjab,the aromatic biryanis of Hyderabad,the herb-laden trattorias of Tuscany. Every dish is a journey, every plate a postcard.

We believe the best meals are crafted from honest ingredients, cooked with genuine care, and shared in good company. That philosophy has made Sunset Serenade a cherished landmark since our very first evening service.
